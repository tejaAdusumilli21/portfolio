About me

I am a skilled Salesforce Developer with expertise in designing, developing, and optimizing scalable solutions on the Salesforce platform. With hands-on experience in Apex, Lightning Web Components (LWC), Visualforce, and Salesforce APIs, I specialize in customizing Salesforce to meet business needs, automate workflows, and enhance user experiences.

I am passionate about building robust, efficient, and user-friendly Salesforce applications that drive business success. Always eager to stay updated with the latest Salesforce advancements and best practices, I strive to deliver high-quality solutions that align with industry standards.

Key Skills & Expertise:

Lightning Web Components (LWC) & Aura Components for dynamic UI development

Apex (Triggers, Batch, Schedulers, Future Methods, Test Classes) for backend customization

SOQL, SOSL & Salesforce Data Model for efficient data management

REST & SOAP APIs, Integrations with third-party applications

Flows, Process Builder, and Declarative Automation

Salesforce Security Model (Profiles, Roles, Sharing Rules, Permission Sets)

Experience Cloud, Service Cloud, Sales Cloud, and Custom Applications

CI/CD, Version Control (Git, Bitbucket), Deployment Tools (Copado, Gearset, SFDX)

What i'm doing

  • Custom Salesforce Development

    I specialize in creating custom Salesforce solutions using Apex, Lightning Web Components (LWC), and Visualforce to address unique business challenges and ensure seamless operations.

  • Salesforce Integrations

    Expert in integrating Salesforce with third-party systems using REST/SOAP APIs and middleware tools like MuleSoft to enhance system connectivity and streamline workflows.
